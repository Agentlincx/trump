What is $TLBTC?

Trump has become the first president to accept payment in BITCOIN for campaign donations.

Trumplovebitcoin is a movement inspired by the 45th President of the United States, Donald Trump. The project aims to create a community-driven cryptocurrency that promotes financial freedom, decentralization, and innovation.

The mission of $TLBTC is to make cryptocurrency accessible to everyone, regardless of their political beliefs or financial status. The project seeks to educate, empower, and connect people through the power of blockchain technology.
